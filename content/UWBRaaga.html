ABOUT US

Raaga UWB Club is a vibrant world music club dedicated to celebrating cultural diversity through music. As a relatively new club, we are fueled by the passion and enthusiasm of our diverse leaders and members. Our club offers a unique platform for individuals from various backgrounds to come together, share their love for music, and showcase their talents. We specialize in singing a wide range of world music genres, creating a harmonious blend of melodies and rhythms from different cultures. In addition to our regular singing sessions, we also host engaging events that promote cultural awareness and unity within our community. At Raaga UWB Club, we believe that music transcends boundaries and fosters connections, making our club a welcoming space for everyone to explore and appreciate the rich tapestry of global musical traditions. Join us as we embark on a musical journey that celebrates diversity and harmony!
